customizing grub2 background

2015-07-06 :: debian, jessie, grub2

customizing the background of grub2 on debian jessie is not documented much, and i have done as follows.

  1. finding out grub2 background resolution

    at grub2 booting menu, type c and enter grub2 shell:

    $ vbeinfo

    the working resolution is the line marked by asterisk.

  2. making your background image

    the image used for the grub2 background must have the same resolution as mentioned above. export the image as tga format.

  3. specifying your image as the grub2 background

    editing file /etc/default/grub and adding the following line:

    GRUB_BACKGROUND="/boot/grub/grub2.tga"

    /boot/grub/grub2.tga is your grub2 background image.

  4. updating grub2 conf

    $ update-grub2
